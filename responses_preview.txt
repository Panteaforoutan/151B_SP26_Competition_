$$
T(t) = T_s + (T_0 - T_s)e^{-kt}
$$

- $ T(t) $ is the temperature at time $ t $,
- $ T_s = 75^\circ F $ is the surrounding room temperature,
- $ T_0 = 185^\circ F $ is the initial temperature of the turkey,

$$
-0.5k = \ln\left(\frac{8}{11}\right) \Rightarrow k = -2 \ln\left(\frac{8}{11}\right) = 2 \ln\left(\frac{11}{8}\right)
